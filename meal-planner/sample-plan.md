# 7-Day Meal Plan — 2 Active Adults
_Mode: **balanced** · Breakfast: off · 1 eat-out swap(s) flagged_

## Batch Prep Strategy (Sunday, ~45 min)
- **Rice:** cook 3 cups dry jasmine rice → ~9 cups cooked. Cool, portion into 3 containers.
- **Chicken:** season 1.2 kg thighs (salt, pepper, a splash of soy + olive oil). Bake 400°F / 205°C, 22–25 min. Slice half, leave half whole.
- **Veg prep:** wash spinach; slice 2 bell peppers; dice 1 onion; mince 4 garlic cloves — store separately.
- **Potatoes:** halve 600 g baby potatoes, microwave 5 min so they're ready to crisp mid-week.
- Result: 3 dinners and 4 lunches come together in <15 min each.

## Intentional Reuse
- **Cooked rice** → Days 1, 2, 3, 7
- **Cooked chicken** → Days 1 dinner, 2 lunch, 5 lunch, 7 dinner
- **Beef skillet leftovers** → Day 3 lunch bowl
- **Spinach + bell peppers** → show up in 6 meals; no half-bag waste
- **Eggs** → bridge any day you don't feel like cooking

## Day 1 — Sunday (prep day)
### Lunch: Egg & veg scramble
**Ingredients (serves 2):**
- Eggs — 6
- Spinach — 2 cups
- Bell pepper, diced — 1
- Onion, diced — 1/2
- Butter — 1 tbsp
**Steps:**
1. Melt butter, soften onion + pepper 2 min.
2. Add spinach, wilt 30 sec.
3. Pour beaten eggs, stir gently until just set. Salt/pepper.
_Why it fits: Fallback meal — works as breakfast, lunch, or lazy dinner._
### Dinner: Garlic soy chicken rice bowl
**Ingredients (serves 2):**
- Chicken thighs, boneless — 500 g
- Jasmine rice, cooked — 3 cups
- Broccoli, florets — 1 head
- Garlic, minced — 4 cloves
- Soy sauce — 3 tbsp
- Olive oil — 1 tbsp
**Steps:**
1. Cube chicken thighs, season with salt/pepper.
2. Sear in olive oil 4–5 min, add garlic, then soy sauce. Toss 1 min.
3. Steam or microwave broccoli 3 min.
4. Serve over warm rice, spoon pan sauce over top.
_Why it fits: The backbone meal — reuses batch rice and cooked chicken._

## Day 2 — Monday
### Lunch: Garlic-soy chicken wraps
**Ingredients (serves 2):**
- Cooked chicken thigh, sliced — 300 g
- Whole wheat wraps — 4
- Spinach — 2 cups
- Bell pepper, sliced — 1
- Soy sauce or hot sauce — to taste
**Steps:**
1. Warm wraps 15 sec in microwave so they fold cleanly.
2. Layer spinach, pepper, chicken. Drizzle soy or hot sauce.
3. Roll tight, slice in half.
_Why it fits: Uses Day-1 batch chicken. 5-min assembly._
### Dinner: Ground beef & potato skillet
**Ingredients (serves 2):**
- Lean ground beef — 500 g
- Baby potatoes, halved — 600 g
- Onion, diced — 1
- Bell pepper, diced — 1
- Garlic, minced — 3 cloves
- Olive oil — 1 tbsp
**Steps:**
1. Microwave potatoes 6 min to soften, then crisp in skillet with olive oil 6–8 min.
2. Push to side; brown beef with onion, pepper, garlic, salt, pepper.
3. Combine, cook 2 more min. Finish with a squeeze of lemon if you like.
_Why it fits: Big satiety per dollar. Leftovers reheat well over rice._

## Day 3 — Tuesday
### Lunch: Savory beef & pepper rice bowl
**Ingredients (serves 2):**
- Leftover beef skillet — ~2 cups
- Jasmine rice, cooked — 2 cups
- Spinach — 2 cups
- Soy sauce — 1 tbsp
**Steps:**
1. Reheat beef skillet in pan, splash of water to loosen.
2. Wilt spinach into it at the end.
3. Serve over rice, soy sauce to finish.
_Why it fits: Zero-effort remix of Day 2 dinner._
### Dinner: Pan-seared salmon with spinach & rice
**Ingredients (serves 2):**
- Salmon fillets — 2 × 180 g
- Spinach, fresh — 200 g
- Jasmine rice, cooked — 2 cups
- Garlic, sliced — 2 cloves
- Butter — 1 tbsp
- Lemon — 1/2
**Steps:**
1. Pat salmon dry, salt/pepper. Sear skin-down 4 min, flip 2–3 min.
2. Remove; in same pan melt butter, toss garlic + spinach until just wilted.
3. Plate over rice, squeeze lemon on salmon.
_Why it fits: Your weekly omega-3 hit. Total time ~15 min._

## Grocery List
### Protein
- Chicken thighs, boneless skinless — 1.2 kg (~2.6 lb)
- Lean ground beef — 500 g (~1.1 lb)
- Salmon fillets — 2 × 180 g
- Eggs — 1 dozen (12)

### Produce
- Broccoli — 2 heads
- Baby potatoes — 1 kg
- Bell peppers — 4
- Spinach — 1 large bag (~300 g)
- Onions — 2 medium
- Garlic — 1 head
- Lemon — 1

### Carbs
- Jasmine rice — 1 kg bag (you will use ~500 g dry)
- Whole wheat wraps — 1 pack (6–8 count)

### Pantry
- Soy sauce (low sodium)
- Olive oil
- Butter
- Salt, pepper
- Hot sauce (optional for wraps)

## Why this plan works
- **Protein every meal.** 6 cooked eggs, 1.2 kg chicken, 500 g beef, and 360 g salmon across the week — puts each of you in the 130–160 g protein/day range without thinking about it.
- **Same shopping list every week if you want.** Reuses 7 core ingredients. Easy to memorize at the store.
- **Leftovers are built into the plan**, not an afterthought. You're never cooking from scratch at 8 PM on a Tuesday.
- **One flagged eat-out slot** (Wednesday) gives you an escape valve without derailing the week.
